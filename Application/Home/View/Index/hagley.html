HAGLEY PARK MOTEL

Hagley Park Motel is situated in a private courtyard setting, in a prime, quiet location. Just a 1-minute walk (50m) to Hagley Park, Chateau on the Park & Christchurch Girls High School. Easy walk to the city centre, Mona Vale, Christchurch Hospital, restaurants, Westfield Shopping Centre, sports fields, tennis and netball courts. 5-minute drive from the railway station, CBS Canterbury Arena, Christchurch AMI Stadium, home of the Canterbury Crusaders, and Addington Raceway. 15 minutes from the airport.

All rooms have LCD flat-screen TVs. Wireless internet is available. Most rooms have a washing machine.

Studios

All with a queen-size bed in the living area, 2 with full kitchen facilities, 1 with tea/coffee-making facilities and microwave, all have private bathrooms.

1-Bedroom Units

Available with a queen-size bed in the bedroom, plus a single bed in the lounge, all these units have full kitchen facilities, washing machine and private bathrooms and all have been recently refurbished.

2-Bedroom Units

Units feature a super king-size bed (3 units), queen-size bed (2 units) in the first bedroom and 2 singles in the second bedroom, separate living area with full kitchen facilities, washing machine (4 units) and private bathroom.
